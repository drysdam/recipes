# Lemon Ice

 - 4c water
 - 3/4 invert sugar
 - 4-6 lemons
 - 1 orange (optional)

Heat water to simmering. Use apple peeler peel zest from lemons and
orange in strips and place strips in water.

Juice the citrus. After removing the peels from the water, pour the
juice into the pan and turn off heat.

When cool, ladle into containers and freeze.
